Board of Directors

2017 – 2018 Chamber Board

The GLC&VC Board of Directors is the legal representative of the Chamber. Its role is to strategically plan and make decisions on behalf of the membership. Board members are chosen because of their interest in the Chamber of Commerce, their standing in the community and their experience and ability in business.
